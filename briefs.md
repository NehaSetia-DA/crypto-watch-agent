# Crypto-watch briefs

A daily log of what moved and what to watch. Newest at the top. Written by the
[Claude routine](https://claude.ai/code/routines/trig_0132eNjqktWyx1H5viJJkrqE)
each morning at ~09:00 IST after the [data fetch action](.github/workflows/fetch-data.yml)
lands fresh prices and headlines.

No investment advice — this surfaces what's happening, you read it.
